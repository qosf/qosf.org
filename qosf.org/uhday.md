# UnitaryHACK day — unitaryHACK 2026

Join us for a local in-person hacking day as part of [unitaryHACK 2026](https://unitaryhack.dev), a global open-source quantum computing hackathon.

## What is unitaryHACK?

unitaryHACK is an annual hackathon organized by the [Unitary Foundation](https://unitary.fund) where participants contribute to open-source quantum computing projects. Running June 3–17, 2026, participants earn bounties by merging pull requests into participating repositories.

## What is a HACKday?

HACKdays are local in-person gatherings during the unitaryHACK period where participants hack together, share ideas, and get support from mentors. The NYU HACKday will feature:

- Opening remarks
- A talk from an IBM speaker
- Open hacking time with mentor support

IBM will be providing speakers and mentors for the day.

## Who should attend?

Students, researchers, and quantum enthusiasts of all experience levels are welcome. Whether you're a first-time contributor or a seasoned open-source developer, there's a place for you.
